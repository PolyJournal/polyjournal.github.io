Current Candidates and Opinion Polls for the First round of Voting

Marine LePen from the National Front (FN) Party has an average of 25.1% in opinion polls. (As of Feb, 2017).

Emmanuel Macron from the En Marche! (EM) party has an average of 21.3% in opinion polls. (As of Feb, 2017).

Francois Fillon from The Republicans has an average of 19.45% in opinion polls. (As of Feb, 2017).

Benoit Hamon from The Socialist Party (PS) has an average of 15.75% in opinion polls. (As of Feb, 2017).

Jean-Luc Melenchon from Unsubmissive France has an average of 10.6% in opion polls. (As of Feb, 2017).

Nicolas Dupont-Aignan from France Arise! has an average of 4.35% in opinion polls. (As of Feb, 2017).

Yannick Jadot from Europe Ecology - The Greens has an average of 3.85% in opinion polls. (As of Feb, 2017).

Phillipe Poutou from The New Anticapitalist Party has an average of 3.3% in opinion polls. (As of Feb, 2017).

Nathalie Arthaud from Worker's Struggle has an average of 0.3% in opinion polls. (As of Feb, 2017).
